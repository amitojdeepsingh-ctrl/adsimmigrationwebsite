What is Express Entry?

Express Entry is Canada's fastest path to permanent residency for skilled workers. Through this program, Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) processes applications in just 6 months or less.

But Express Entry is competitive. Your profile must be optimized for maximum points. Small mistakes—missing documents, poor language test scores, incomplete work experience details—cost you points and can result in rejection.

That's where we come in.

Who Qualifies for Express Entry?

✓

Education

Bachelor's degree or higher (or equivalent)

✓

Work Experience

At least 1 year of skilled work (or 2 years Canadian)

✓

Language Skills

CLB 6+ in English or French (IELTS, TOEFL, or TEF)

✓

Age

Typically 18–45 (younger = more points)

Express Entry has three pathways:

1

Federal Skilled Worker Program (FSWP)

Most popular, for skilled workers without Canadian experience

2

Canadian Experience Class (CEC)

For those with Canadian work experience

3

Federal Skilled Trades Program (FSTP)

For trade professionals

The CRS Points System

Express Entry uses the Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) to score applications. Your score determines your ranking in the pool. Recent draws require 500–530 CRS points.

Our Express Entry Process

1

Profile Assessment (1 hour)

We analyze your background, calculate your estimated CRS score, and discuss realistic timelines.

2

Document Preparation (2–3 weeks)

We help organize and verify all required documents: education credentials, language test results, work experience letters, and financial proof.

3

Profile Creation (1 week)

We optimize your Express Entry profile in the IRCC system to present your strongest candidacy.

4

Pool Submission (1 day)

Your profile enters the pool and waits for an invitation draw. Most invitations are issued every 2 weeks.

5

Invitation & Application (2–4 months)

Once invited, you have 60 days to submit a full application. We review all documents, ensure accuracy, and submit on your behalf.

6

Processing & Approval (2–4 months)

IRCC processes your complete application. We monitor progress and respond to any requests for additional information.

Total Timeline: 4–6 months from first consultation to permanent residency approval.
